Music School Management:

This module works on both community and enterprise editions

Using this module, you can easily manage a complete music school, add classes and generate lessons automatically based on repeat rules, also manage your instruments easily and effectively. You can invoice the lessons to each student based on the classes they select.


Music Institute/School Management Features

Create students / teachers

Easily manage classes / lessons

manage your inventory of instruments (piano, drums, guitars…etc)

get complete reports on the attendance of students

Generate invoices and track payments based on the number of lessons

Multi-Company Support
